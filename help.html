How do I play?
Press the space bar to bounce the ball and try to avoid hitting anything! If you are on a touch only device, tap on the pointer icon in the top right corner.

Features

Skins / Collections Menu

You can change what your ball looks like by clicking one of the three skins on the top. If you'd like to see a full category of our skins that you can use, click on the three dots on the top bar to open the Collections Menu! New skins are added frequently!

Levels Menu

The Levels Menu is a new feature where you can instantly switch to the level you want, or pick up where you left off! Simply click on the levels button in the bottom left corner and then select which level you would like to play from the menu!

Obstacles

Tubes

Tubes are non-moving obsticles which can be in the in the air or attached to the ceiling or floor.

Moving Tubes

These tube shaped obsticles move from top to bottom. Try not to it them!

Moving Ball

These round shaped obsticles move from top to bottom. Try not to it them!
